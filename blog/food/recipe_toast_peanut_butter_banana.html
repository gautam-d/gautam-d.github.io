~ Recipe ~ Toast with Peanut Butter & Banana ~


...

Prep Time:

5 mins

Cook Time:

5 mins

Total Time:

10 mins

Ingredients:


~ Item ~
~ Quantity ~
~ Recommended Brand ~

bread, multigrain

2 slices

peanut butter, chunky

4 tbsp

banana

1

cinammon (ground)

2 dashes

Servings:

1

Step 1:


Peel and chop the banana into slices.



Step 2:


Place the bread in a toaster, and heat until it is warmed and lightly browned. Do not toast all the way.


Step 3:


After lightly toasting, apply 2 tbsp of peanut butter per slice of bread. Then add half the banana slices per each slice of bread. Finally, sprinkle a dash of cinnamon on over the banana slices



Step 4:


Place the bread in an oven toaster and heat for about 2-3 minutes. Make sure not to burn the toast.


Step 5:


Remove the toast from the oven toaster, cut each piece of bread into 2, and allow to cool. Then, enjoy the sweet, gooey goodness you just created! Pair with a cup of tea, coffee or milk.

~ Recipe ~ Masala Chai (microwave) ~


Masala chai, or "spiced tea" for the uninitiated, is a popular beverage comsumed religiously by Indians. It is typically prepared on the stove-top and is made with a lot of milk. However, my version is made in the microwave, which makes preparation and clean up a lot easier. I also prefer to use less milk and focus on the "chai" and "masala". Hardcore chai drinkers may consider my deviations blasphemous, but, hey, don't knock it 'til you try it.


~~~~~~~~~~

Prep Time:

5 mins

Cook Time:

15 mins

Total Time:

20 mins

Ingredients:


~ Item ~
~ Quantity ~
~ Recommended Brand ~

tea, loose

2 tsp

Brooke Bond Taj Mahal

tea masala

1 tsp

Badshah Kamal Tea Masala

sugar

2 tsp

ginger (freshly grated)

1 tbsp

water

2 cup

milk

1/4 cup


Servings:

 2 cups

~~~~~~~~~~

Step 1:


In a microwaveable beaker, add water and grated ginger. Place beaker in microwave, cover with lid and heat to boil.


Step 2:


Add loose tea, tea masala and sugar. Place beaker in microwave and bring to boil once more. Allow to steep for 10 mins.


Step 3:


After steeping, bring to boil in microwave once more.


Step 4:


Remove tea from microwave and pour into a cup with a strainer. Add milk. And enjoy!

~ Recipe ~ Egg, Cheese & Zaatar Manaeesh ~


Manaeesh is Middle Eastern dish similar to pizza, consisting of a flat bread with variety of options for toppings. One of the most common toppings on manaeesh is zaatar. Zaatar is an Arabic blend of ground spices that includes thyme, oregano, marjoram, sumac, salt, and toasted sesame seeds. I used to enjoy eating cheese & zaatar manaeesh from the local restaurants while growing up in the Middle East.


In order to bring some tastes of my childhood back, I've come up with an improvised version of cheese & zaataar manaeesh that also includes eggs and vegetables. While not being the traditional recipe, I still enjoy this healthy and easy-to-make version.

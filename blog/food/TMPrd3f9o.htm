~ Recipe ~ Egg & Cheese Mulitas ~


Mulitas are basically mini-quesidillas made with small-sized tortillas. This recipe uses an egg, cheese and vegetable filling.


You can also find my recipe for bean & cheese mulitas here >>.


~~~~~~~~~~

Prep Time:

15 mins

Cook Time:

15 mins

Total Time:

30 mins

~~~~~~~~~~

Ingredients:


~ Item ~
~ Quantity ~
~ Recommended Brand ~

corn tortillas, white or yellow, 6" dia

8

eggs

3 (3 whites, 1 yolk)

onions (chopped)

1/4 cup

red or orange bell peppers (chopped)

1/4 cup

italian parsley (chopped)

1/4 cup

avocado (coarsley chopped)

1 medium size

cheese, mozarella (shredded)

1/2 cup

chilli garlic sauce

1 tsp

Huy Fong Chilli Garlic Sauce

lemon juice

1 tbsp

salt

1/2 tsp

black pepper, coarse ground

1/2 tsp

oil, canola or olive

1 tbsp



~~~~~~~~~~


Servings:

  2  (4 mulitas, 2 per person)

~~~~~~~~~~

Step 1:


In a bowl, add the eggs (3 whites, 1 yolk), salt, black pepper, and chilli garlic paste. Whip the mixture together and set a side.


Step 2:


In a saucepan, add cooking oil and heat on high. Add the onions and bell peppers, and sautee on medium-high until golden brown.


Step 3:


Reduce the heat to low, and add the egg mixture to the saucepan. Cook on medium-low, stirring frequently until the eggs have cooked.


Step 4:


Add the italian parsley and lemon juice. Stir and cook for a few minutes on medium-high. Turn off the heat, cover, and set aside.


Step 5:


On a large frying pan, place 2 corn tortillas and heat on medium-high. Once tortillas have browned on one side, flip over and brown the other side.


Step 6:


Bring heat to low. On one tortilla, add 1/8 cup of shredded cheese. Then, add quarter of the prepared egg and vegetable filling on top of the cheese. Next, add quarter amount of the avaocado pieces. Finally, place the other tortilla on top.


Step 7:


Continue to heat mulitas until the cheese melts, but make sure not to burn the tortilla. Then, flip the mulitas once to heat other side.


Step 8:


Remove from frying pan and cut mulitas in half.


Step 9:


Repeat steps 5 - 8 with the remaining ingredients to make 4 mulitas in total. Serve and enjoy!

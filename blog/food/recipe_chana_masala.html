~ Recipe ~ Chana Masala (Chickpeas Curry) ~


...


~~~~~~~~~~

Prep Time:

15 mins

Cook Time:

30 mins

Total Time:

45 mins

~~~~~~~~~~

Ingredients:


~ Item ~
~ Quantity ~
~ Recommended Brand ~

chickpeas (canned), rinsed

16 oz

tomato sauce (canned)

8 oz

onions, chopped

1/2 cup

cilantro / italian parsley, chopped

1/4 cup

coconut, shredded (fresh or frozen)

1/4 cup

ginger, grated

1 tsp

garlic, minced

1 tsp

mustard seeds

1 tsp

cumin seeds

1 tsp

salt

1 - 2 tsp

black pepper

1/2 tsp

cayenne pepper

1/2 tsp

turmeric powder

1 tsp

garam masala powder

1/2 tsp

canola oil

2 tbsp

water

1/2 cup




Servings:

 2

~~~~~~~~~~

Step 1:


In a saucepan, add canola oil and heat on high. First, add the mustard seeds. Once the mustard seeds start cooking and lighten in color, add the cumin seeds. As the mustard seeds start to crackle, lower heat.


Step 2:


Once the cumin seeds have turned brown in color, add the onions, ginger and garlic. Stir and cook on medium-high until the onions turn translucent.


Step 3:


Add the chickpeas and ground spices (salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, turmeric powder, and garam masala powder). Stir and cook on medium-high for 3-5 minutes.


Step 4:


Add the tomato sauce, stir and continue to cook for another for 3-5 minutes.


Step 5:


Add the water, stir and cook on high heat until it comes to a boil. Taste gravy and add ground spices to adjust as required.


Step 6:


Once boiling, bring heat down to low, cover and leave to simmer for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Turn off heat and leave covered for about 5 minutes.


Step 7:


Serve chana masala on a bed of rice or quinoa. Garnish with cilantro / parsley and shredded coconut, and enjoy!


Additional Notes:


  • To prepare more servings, simply scale up the ingredient quantiies (i.e. use 2x the ingredients for 4 servings, 3x the ingredients for 6 servings, etc.).

~ Volunteer Project: "George, My War Hero" ~


In 2009/2010, I did some volunteer work at a center for people with blindness and visually impairment. I worked with an elderly client with visual impairment to help her write and self-publish her memoir. The memoir is about her late husband, who had faught in WWII, and their experiences during that period of time in their lives. The book is titled "George, My War Hero".

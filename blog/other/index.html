~ Volunteering Project: "George, My War Hero ~


In 2009/2010, I did some volunteer work at a center for people who are blind and visually impaired. I worked with an elderly client with visual impairment to help her write and self-publish her memoir. The memoir is about her late husband, who had faught in WWII, and their experiences during that period of time in their lives.
